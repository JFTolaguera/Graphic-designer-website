Who I am

I'm Jeff, a graphic designer working on Android & Windows Phone, front-end web developer, webdesigner, and Civil Engineering student.

Drawing and photography have always been hobbies of mine. Few years ago, I discovered CAD and graphic design, which I learned the ropes by myself. I've also been a big fan of Android since Ice Cream Sandwich (2011), and I love designing stuff on this platform.

When I design, I'm a thinker. Whatever the product, I pay attention to UX, what does the user expect. I spend hours drawing sketches, musing over how to build the most delightful experience. And I spend more designing a great interface with a strong brand identity.

What I do

On Android :

Android design. I work with developers on Android applications, exploring Google Material Design. I focus on User Experience, and create UI mockups, illustrations, product/system icons, 9-patches, graphic features and more. I do the same on Windows Phone, which features Modern UI.

On the web :

Web design and front-end development. I design mockups, wireframes and other deliverables, paying attention to ergonomics. Furthermore, I'm constantly developping my coding skills, making small websites with HTML5 · CSS3 · Javascript, focusing on responsive design and performance. I'm not a back-end developer.

And more :

If I'm not designing, you can find me shooting street photography, travelling, running, playing drums or studying.
